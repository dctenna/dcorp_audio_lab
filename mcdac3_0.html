McDAC 3,0

That project is how to get excellent sound with modern tecnologies. McDAC 3.0 is based on high quality Digital to Analog converter chip AK4399 organically combined with specially designed high-end tube output stage.

The power supply is made as separate block for minimizing its influence on the main unit.

The device has USB 1.1 standard input so you won’t need special drivers installed. Either ballanced and unbalanced outputs can be used. DAC produces 1V RMS signal amplitude at 0dB input.
